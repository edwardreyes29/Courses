III. Table styles
    Table Classes: 
        * table 
        * table-dark (inverse table)

    Style Classes:
        * table-striped: creates slightly diff colors for each
        * table-border 
        * table-borderless
        * table-hover

    Head Colors:
        * thead-light 
        * thead-dark

    Color Options:
        * table-COLOR: TRs & TDs
            active | primary | secondary | success | danger | warning | info | light | dark
            - lighter than regular
        * bg-COLOR: Trs & TDs
            primary | success | danger | warning | info
        * text-COLOR: for text
            primary | secondary | success | danger | warning | info | light | dark

    Size/Responsive:
        * table-sm
        * table-responsive(-BP)
            BP: sm | md | lg | xl
